Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LabDash

What is LabDash and what services does it offer?

LabDash is a comprehensive healthcare management platform offering Laboratory Management System (LIMS), PetCare veterinary services, Pharmaceutical product sales, and Certified Lab Testing services. We help labs, clinics, and hospitals streamline their operations with our all-in-one solution.

What is the Welcome Bonus?

When you sign up and log in for the first time, you'll receive an exclusive Welcome Bonus! Get â‚¹500 instant credit, flat 20% off on all lab tests, free home sample collection on your first order, and priority customer support for 30 days. Start your healthcare journey with amazing benefits!

Is my patient data secure?

Absolutely! We use bank-grade 256-bit encryption and are HIPAA compliant. All data is stored on secure cloud servers with regular backups. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I integrate LabDash with my existing systems?

Yes! LabDash offers REST API integration with most popular lab equipment, hospital management systems, and payment gateways. Our Enterprise plan includes custom integration support with dedicated technical assistance.

Do you provide training for staff?

Yes! We provide comprehensive onboarding training for all staff members via video tutorials, documentation, and live sessions. Professional and Enterprise plans include dedicated training sessions and ongoing support.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, UPI, and bank transfers. For annual plans, we also offer EMI options. Enterprise clients can opt for invoice-based billing.
